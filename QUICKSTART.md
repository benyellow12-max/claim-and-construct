## Play Immediately

1. **Open in Browser**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Enter Your Name**: Type your player name and click "Start Game"
3. **Build Your First Base**: Click on any location on the map and click "Build Base"

That's it! Your game progress is automatically saved.

## What to Do First

1. **Explore the Map**: Use mouse to pan, scroll to zoom
2. **Build Multiple Bases**: Each base generates coins and troops
3. **Upgrade Your Bases**: Click on a base and upgrade it to increase production
4. **Level Up**: You gain XP with each base built

## Tips

- **Starting Resources**: 1000 coins + 50 troops
- **Build Cost**: 100 coins + 20 troops per base
- **Passive Income**: Base production starts immediately after building
- **Upgrades**: Level your bases to boost production (expensive!)
- **Mobilize**: Use "Locate Me" button to center map on your position

## For Better Experience

If geolocation or PWA features don't work in your browser, run a local server:

**Windows (PowerShell):**
```powershell
python -m http.server 8000
# Then visit http://localhost:8000
```

**Mac/Linux (Terminal):**
```bash
python3 -m http.server 8000
# Then visit http://localhost:8000
```

## Game Data

All your game progress is stored locally in your browser. If you clear your browser cache, you'll lose your progress!

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- Type player name and press **Enter** to start game
